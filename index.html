My dearest Kirti,

Okay, I know what you're going to say. Happ. Already. Before you've even read the whole thing. But no — not today. Today you sit here and feel every single word. Deal with it. 🖤

Another year older, and you somehow just keep getting more you. Bolder. Warmer. Funnier. More chaotically wonderful. I don't know how you do it but I'm convinced it's a superpower.

You're the friend who makes the late nights worth it. The one I can talk to about absolutely nothing until the clock says 3am and we both have things to do tomorrow but neither of us cares. Those hours are some of my favourite ones.

And Kirti — you know what we need to talk about. All. The. Food. Every time we see something delicious online, every restaurant we've bookmarked, every "we HAVE to try this someday" moment — I am holding you to every single one of them. We dreamt together about eating the most delicious things in the world, and that is a promise I intend to keep. Me, you, and a table full of the best food we can find. Non-negotiable.

But more than the food, more than the laughs, more than all the inside jokes — what I love most about you is how genuinely, quietly, deeply loving you are. You don't make a show of it. You just show up. You just care. And that makes you one of the rarest people I know.

You are sweet and you are fierce and you are an absolute queen, Kirti. The kind the universe doesn't make very often.

So today — no happ's. Just love. So much of it. All of it. 🖤

with everything, Ayush
